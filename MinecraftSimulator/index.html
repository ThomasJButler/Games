How to Save and Load Worlds

Saving a World:

  1. Click the "Save World" button.
  2. The text in the box above represents your current world state.
  3. Copy this text and save it somewhere safe. This is your "world save text".

Loading a World:

  1. Click the "Load World" button.
  2. Paste your previously saved "world save text" into the box above.
  3. Click "Confirm Load" to rebuild your saved world.

Note: The world save text contains information about block types and their positions. Make sure to copy the entire text to ensure a complete world load.
