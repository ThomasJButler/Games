Welcome to PacSnake!

PacSnake is the ultimate maze muncher, combining the classic gameplay of Pac-Man with the growing challenge of Snake. Navigate through the maze, eat dots to grow longer, and watch out for those pesky ghosts!

How to Play:

  • Use the arrow keys to control your PacSnake
  • Eat the blue dots to grow and increase your score
  • Collect the large red power pellets to temporarily turn the tables on the ghosts
  • Avoid the ghosts when you're not powered up, or it's game over!

Power-Ups:

When you eat a power pellet, you'll become invincible for a short time. During this period, you can eat the ghosts for bonus points! But be quick - the power-up won't last forever!

Ghosts:

  • Blinky (Red): The leader of the ghost pack, always on your tail!
  • Pinky (Pink): Tries to ambush you by predicting your moves
  • Inky (Blue): Works with Blinky to corner you
  • Clyde (Orange): The wildcard, sometimes helpful, sometimes not

Can you outmaneuver the ghosts and set a new high score? Give it a try and see how long you can survive in the world of PacSnake!
